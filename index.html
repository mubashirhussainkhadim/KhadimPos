Description

Khadim POS is a powerful and user-friendly Point of Sale system designed to streamline and enhance your business operations. Ideal for various environments including mobile shops and restaurants, Khadim POS offers comprehensive tools for managing inventory, sales, and financial transactions. Enjoy features like barcode printing, multi-currency support, and robust reporting capabilities, all within a customizable and intuitive interface.

Features

Product Management

Manage your inventory with ease, including multiple images for each product.

Barcode Printing

Generate and print barcodes to streamline sales and inventory management.

Stock Management

Track and manage stock levels efficiently with real-time updates and alerts.

Quotation Management

Create and send professional quotations directly from the system.

Purchase and Sales Management

Efficiently handle purchases, sales, and returns with our integrated solution.

Expense Management

Track and manage business expenses to keep your budget in check.

Customer & Supplier Management

Maintain detailed records and manage interactions with customers and suppliers.

User Management

Define roles and permissions to control user access and functionality.

Multi-Currency Support

Handle transactions in various currencies to cater to an international audience.

Unit Management

Manage different units of measurement for products, enhancing inventory accuracy.

Backup & Restore

Ensure data safety with easy backup and restore options for peace of mind.

Detailed Reports

Generate insightful reports to make data-driven business decisions.
